Mutual Funds

Innealta Capital offers two distinct and unique mutual funds, rooted in core academic principals and implemented through use of a proprietary quantitative framework. The funds target a broad, unbiased exposure to sectors, indsutries, countries, and regions, in accordance with its mandate. The funds are available at most major custodians and broker/dealers.
learn more

Separately Managed Accounts

Innealta offers four broad categories of separately managed accounts ranging from pure fixed income, U.S. equities, international equities, and global multi-asset class portfolios. Innealta’s SMAs are available through Envestnet, LPL, most major custodians, as well as many other platforms.
