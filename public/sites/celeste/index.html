J. Celeste Lay

Associate Professor &
Sr. Associate Dean of Newcomb-Tulane College,
Tulane University

I am an associate professor in the department of political science at Tulane University. My research focuses on American political behavior, political socialization and education policy.

In NTC, I am the Sr. Assoc Dean of Academic Affairs, with responsibility over the Honors Program, the Center for Global Education, the Center for Academic Equity, Academic Integrity, ROTC, and the Core Curriculum.
