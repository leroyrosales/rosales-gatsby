The Sacred Rest Stop

The Sacred Rest Stop is a community meeting and meditation space located on 10 acres of bountiful Texas farmland 15 minutes east of downtown Austin.

The genesis of The Sacred Rest Stop came from a simple recognition that people are yearning for a place to physically gather together that provides easily accessible ways to develop deeper intimacy with themselves, their community, the natural world and That Which Holds Us All.

Now is the time. Check out our events and classes! Join us!
