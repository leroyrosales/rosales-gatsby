MESSAGE FROM THE PRESIDENT | Hugh Rice
The National Academy of Construction is composed of 254 distinguished leaders from every segment of the engineering and construction industry. The Academy, whose members are selected through a rigorous peer nomination and election process, represents all sectors of the built environmentâ€”owners, contractors, designers, government agencies, professional service firms, and academia. Our mission is to select, honor, and recognize those individuals who have made extraordinary contributions to our industry.

As NAC members, these acknowledged construction industry leaders are presented with a unique opportunity to help the industry and the United States make important decisions on issues with far-reaching effects: how to provide a motivated, technically savvy workforce, how to improve the industry's image, how to make safety the number one priority always, how strategies for future success can be planned now to benefit the entire world tomorrow, and many others.

The National Academy of Construction is a premier group of leaders. This diverse expertise stands ready as a collective voice for the industry. The strategies that will be needed going forward, in infrastructure, in capital projects, and in business both nationally and globally, need the guidance that Academy leadership represents. Our aim is to utilize the knowledge, experience, and talents of our members in support of the industry and the nation.
