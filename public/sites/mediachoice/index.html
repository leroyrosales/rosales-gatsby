    Let Our Billboards Work For You

Media Choice is an outdoor advertising company headquartered in Austin, Texas that’s helped connect advertisers with audiences for more than 20 years.

Our portfolio includes static and digital outdoor advertising displays.

Our primary markets are located across Texas (Central, South, East, and Rio Grande Valley) and southeastern Louisiana. You can also spot our billboards in Oklahoma, Missouri, and Arkansas.

WHY OUTDOOR

CONSTANT EXPOSURE

Beyond DVRs, ad blockers, ad-free music, and declining print. Outdoor delivers exposure, reinforcement, and results.

COST EFFECTIVE

Cost per thousand impressions (CPM) is low compared to other media. Outdoor brings high value, at low cost.

REACH AND REPETITION

Outdoor provides total reach and repetition with your audience. Perfect to build your brand, and deliver your message.

HIGH IMPACT

Outdoor offers a blank canvas for high impact visual messages. A bold presence that commands attention for your message.
